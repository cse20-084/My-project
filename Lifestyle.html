X-line Adventures

The new Xline is the world’s longest urban zipline, set in Dubai Marina and measuring 1 KM in length. It is also one of the steepest and fastest with an incline of 16 degrees and travelling at an average of 80 KM per hour. Take advantage of the Special Summer Rates: Solo ride for 1 person is AED 399 and Double Trouble ticket for 2 people is AED 699. The Summer Rates will run from June 3rd until September 30th 2020. You must be aged 12 to 60 years old. Riders under 18 must be accompanied by a legal guardian on the appointment date. You must be a minimum height of 130 cm, weigh 50-100 kg, and in a good medical condition. Make sure you read our Terms and Conditions to see all the requirements to ride. The XLine meeting and check-in point is at the Dubai Marina Mall, XLine booth on level P. There is no shuttle bus to the Marina Mall organized by XDUBAI but you can take the metro (Marina Mall Metro Station) or taxi. There is also plenty of parking available for you at the mall.You can find the exact location of the mall here The XLine is built and operated by highly qualified and globally certified teams. Our team carries out daily testing and regular maintenance of the line, making sure safety is always first. Alternatively, you can visit the XDubai Shops in Dubai Marina Mall or Dubai Mall and purchase and purchase a gift voucher.

Dubai Atlantis Discovery

One of the best places for scuba diving in Dubai, Jumeirah Beach offers small sites for beginners under its turquoise waters and enchanting marine life. Dubai being a seaport is littered with self-contained ecosystems in the wrecks of the Persian Gulf. Some are depths that only technical divers are qualified for but, for the most part, a portion of the wreck, the MV Dara is accessible to advanced divers. In the Gulf of Oman, Fujairah is a favorite site featuring warm water, bleached sand and tropical fish. Sharm rock and Martini rock are pinnacles in the area that are a natural aquarium for marine species. The pinnacles are adorned with pink and purple corals. Take advantage of Dubai’s clear seas to gain your PADI Open Water scuba dive certification with this practical 3-day course. Prebook to secure the course time slots that suit you, and once in Dubai, enjoy confined water training followed by four open-water dives to qualify. Choose to train over any three days and complete the course’s e-learning modules (additional cost) before you travel or between dives—it’s up to you.

Sky Diving In Dubai

Life is about taking a leap of faith, and sometimes it's about taking that leap from 13,500 feet with your legs dangling precariously from the edge of an aircraft. If you’re looking for an adrenalin rush, and an exceptional sense of accomplishment, nothing beats skydiving. Nothing you do will ever thrill you like the experience of skydiving and chances are, you would immediately want to do it all over again. For Indians, skydiving in Dubai is probably the best way to experience Dubai’s flamboyant sights. Important things you need to know about skydiving in Dubai is that,those who wish to go sky diving in Dubai, must be above 18 years of age,must weigh below 100kg for safety reasons and are required to sign a liability waiver form that lists out the risks involved in the process of sky diving Weather plays an important role in the success of a dive, so bad weather may lengthen the time taken for the entire activity. On a good day, you'll have to spare at least three hours for the entire diving experience
